How do we measure controversy for a webpage?
A webpage is controversial if its related Wikipedia entries are controversial and have been modified back and forth a lot by human editors (more details).

Highly On: the Wikipedia article would have contributed to constructing knowledge of the webpage./ I would read this Wikipedia article to learn more about the webpage.
Slightly On: this Wikipedia article covers a certain topic of the webpage, but it is not the main topic.
Slightly Off: this Wikipedia article is somewhat related to the webpage in a broad sense, but not directly related.
Highly Off: this Wikipedia article has nothing to do with the webpage.
